Build Digital Trust at Every Touchpoint

Secure your customer journey from end to end with advanced identity verification, fraud prevention, and seamless, passwordless authentication.

Security vs. Experience: A False Choice

In the digital economy, your user login and onboarding process is your front door. Too much friction drives customers away, but too little security invites fraud. Businesses are forced to walk a tightrope between providing a seamless user experience and protecting against sophisticated threats like account takeover.

Account Takeover (ATO):

Credential stuffing and phishing attacks lead to compromised accounts, financial loss, and reputational damage.

Customer Abandonment:

Complex password requirements and clunky multi-factor authentication (MFA) cause user frustration and lead to lost business.

Sophisticated Spoofing:

Advanced threats like deepfakes and presentation attacks can bypass traditional identity verification methods during onboarding.

Our Approach: Frictionless Security, Unquestionable Identity

Trescudo’s Identity & Fraud Prevention solution, powered by the innovative VU Security platform, is designed to solve the security vs. experience dilemma. We believe you can have both.

Our approach combines advanced biometrics, behavioral analysis, and AI-driven risk scoring to create a dynamic, adaptive trust model. This allows you to provide a seamless, passwordless experience for legitimate users while automatically stepping up verification for high-risk actions or suspicious behaviors.

A Complete Customer Identity & Access Management (CIAM) Platform

Secure the entire user lifecycle, from initial onboarding to every login and transaction.

Secure Digital Onboarding

Verify user identities with confidence using document capture (OCR), facial proof-of-life detection, and validation against government databases, all while ensuring KYC/AML compliance.

Passwordless Authentication

Eliminate passwords entirely. Allow users to log in seamlessly and securely using facial biometrics, voice recognition, QR codes, or magic links, drastically reducing friction and ATO risk.

Adaptive Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Intelligently apply friction only when needed. Our risk engine analyzes user behavior, device posture, and location to trigger step-up authentication only for high-risk scenarios.

Advanced Fraud Detection

Detect and block fraud in real time. Our platform analyzes behavioral biometrics—like typing cadence and device tilt—and combines it with device fingerprinting to identify and stop fraudulent activity.

More Than CIAM. A Platform Advantage.

Our Identity & Fraud Prevention solution is a core pillar of the Trescudo platform, integrating seamlessly with our other best-in-class offerings.

  • Protect Your Applications:

    Combine robust user authentication with our WAF and Anti-DDoS solutions from NSFOCUS to provide a layered defence for your customer-facing applications.

  • Secure Backend Infrastructure:

    Ensure that the cloud infrastructure and APIs supporting your identity platform are secure with agentless scanning and vulnerability management from Orca Security and Vicarius.

  • Powered by Managed Services:

    Let our expert team monitor for sophisticated fraud patterns and respond to identity-based threats 24/7, augmenting your internal fraud and security teams.
