Endpoint Security (XDR)

  • Home
  • Endpoint Security (XDR)

Autonomous Protection for Every Endpoint

Stop breaches faster than ever with AI-powered Endpoint Security (XDR) that prevents, detects, and responds to threats at machine speed, across your entire enterprise.

Your Endpoints Are the New Perimeter

With a distributed workforce, legacy antivirus is no longer enough. Endpoints—from laptops and servers to cloud workloads—are the primary target for sophisticated attacks like ransomware. Your defence must be faster, smarter, and more automated than ever.

Sophisticated Malware:

Fileless attacks, zero-day exploits, and polymorphic malware easily bypass traditional, signature-based antivirus solutions.

The Distributed Workforce:

Users and devices are everywhere, connecting from outside the traditional network perimeter and expanding your attack surface.

Slow Human Response:

Security teams are overwhelmed. By the time an analyst can investigate an alert, the damage from a ransomware attack is already done.

Our Approach: Autonomous, AI-Powered Defence

Trescudo’s endpoint security solution, powered by the market-leading SentinelOne Singularity™ Platform, replaces outdated antivirus with a single, autonomous agent. It uses advanced AI models to predict and block malicious behaviors in real-time, on the device itself, without needing constant cloud connectivity.

This isn't just about detecting threats; it's about ending them instantly. Our platform provides automated, one-click remediation and can even roll back an endpoint to its pre-attack state, neutralizing ransomware in seconds.

From EPP to EDR to XDR: A Unified Platform

Consolidate multiple endpoint agents and capabilities into a single, powerful platform.

AI Endpoint Protection
(EPP)

Prevent threats at the door. Our AI-powered engine blocks malware, ransomware, and zero-day exploits before they can execute, providing a powerful first line of defence.

Automated Remediation & Ransomware Rollback

Act instantly. With a single click, our platform can kill processes, quarantine files, disconnect a device from the network, and even surgically roll back any unauthorized changes made during an attack.

Endpoint Detection & Response
(EDR)

See everything. Our platform provides deep visibility into all endpoint activity, with easy-to-understand context and automated root-cause analysis that simplifies threat hunting and investigation.

Extended Detection & Response
(XDR)

Go beyond the endpoint. Our platform ingests data from other security layers—like cloud, identity, and network—to provide a unified, enterprise-wide view of complex attacks, enabling faster and more effective response.

More Than an Endpoint Solution.
A Platform Advantage.

Our Endpoint Security solution is a core pillar of the Trescudo platform, integrating seamlessly with our other best-in-class offerings.

  • Unified with Cloud Security:

    Correlate endpoint threats with cloud activity. See if a compromised endpoint is attempting to access sensitive cloud resources, all within a single view.

  • Powered by Managed Services (MDR):

    Augment your team with our 24/7 SOC analysts who use our XDR platform to hunt for threats, manage alerts, and respond on your behalf, acting as a true force multiplier.

  • Informed by Vulnerability Management:

    Prioritize patching and remediation efforts by understanding which vulnerabilities are being actively targeted on your endpoints, bridging the gap between detection and prevention.
