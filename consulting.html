Consulting & Advisory

  • Home
  • Consulting & Advisory

Clarity and Confidence in a Complex World

Strategic Consulting & Advisory services designed to align your cybersecurity program with your business objectives and navigate the evolving threat and regulatory landscape.

Your Toughest Challenges Aren't Just Technical

Effective cybersecurity leadership requires more than just implementing tools. It demands a deep understanding of business risk, regulatory pressures, and resource optimization. Our advisory services are built to address these strategic pillars.

Aligning Security & Business Goals:

Translating technical risk into business impact to justify investments and gain executive buy-in.

Navigating Regulatory Complexity:

Keeping pace with evolving mandates like NIS2 and DORA while maintaining operational efficiency.

Optimizing Security Investments:

Moving from a reactive, tool-based approach to a strategic, risk-based program that maximizes ROI.

Our Advisory Philosophy: Business-First, Vendor-Agnostic

Our consulting engagements always start with your business objectives, not with a product. We seek to understand your unique operational realities, risk appetite, and strategic goals. This allows us to provide pragmatic, actionable advice that is tailored to your organization and independent of any single technology.

Powered by the deep expertise of Cronix, our advisory team brings over 20 years of cross-industry experience to every engagement.

Our Core Advisory Offerings

A suite of services designed to provide strategic support at every level of your organization.

Virtual CISO (vCISO)

Get the benefit of executive-level cybersecurity leadership without the overhead. Our vCISO service provides strategic planning, board-level reporting, budget justification, and program management to mature your security posture.

Risk & Compliance Assessments

Comprehensive gap analyses and readiness assessments for key regulations and standards, including NIS2, DORA, ISO 27001, and more. We deliver a clear, actionable roadmap to compliance.

Cloud Security & Architecture Review

De-risk your cloud transformation. Our experts review your cloud architecture (AWS, Azure, GCP) for security best practices, cost-efficiency, and resilience, ensuring your cloud environment is built on a secure foundation.

IT/OT Convergence Strategy

For industrial clients, we provide specialized advisory services to help you develop a unified security program that safely and effectively governs both your corporate IT and operational technology (OT) environments.

Why Partner with Trescudo for Advisory?

Our advice is grounded in decades of real-world, hands-on experience.

  • Practitioner-Led Guidance:

    Our consultants are seasoned practitioners, not just theorists. Our advice is pragmatic, actionable, and born from over 20 years of experience in the trenches.

  • Backed by a World-Class Platform:

    Our strategic advice is informed by the real-time data and capabilities of our curated technology platform, allowing us to validate our recommendations with empirical evidence.

  • A Focus on Enablement:

    Our goal is to empower your team. We focus on knowledge transfer and building internal capabilities, ensuring our partnership creates lasting value for your organization.
