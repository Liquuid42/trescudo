Managed Security Services (MSSP)

  • Home
  • Managed Security Services (MSSP)

Your 24/7/365 Cyber Defence Partner

Expert-led Managed Security Services that act as a seamless extension of your team, providing the people, processes, and platform to secure your business around the clock.

Your Team Can't Be Everywhere at Once

Building and maintaining an effective in-house security operations center is a significant challenge. Most organizations struggle with a persistent talent shortage, overwhelming alert volumes, and the impossibility of providing true 24/7 coverage.

The Cybersecurity Skills Gap:

Hiring, training, and retaining elite security analysts is difficult and expensive.

Alert Fatigue & Burnout:

Your team is drowning in low-fidelity alerts from dozens of tools, leading to missed critical threats.

The 24/7 Threat Cycle:

Attackers don't operate on a 9-to-5 schedule. An attack at 3 AM can go undetected until morning.

Our Approach: Your Team, Amplified

Trescudo’s MSSP is not about outsourcing alerts; it’s about augmenting your capabilities. We act as a true partner and a seamless extension of your team. Our approach combines the deep, hands-on expertise of Cronix's security analysts with the intelligent automation of our curated technology platform.

This creates a powerful feedback loop: our expert analysts make the platform smarter, and the platform’s AI-driven insights make our analysts more effective—delivering unparalleled protection for your business.

Our Core Managed Security Offerings

A suite of services designed to provide comprehensive, around-the-clock protection for your entire enterprise.

24/7/365 Security Operations Center (SOC)

The core of our service. Our global SOC provides continuous monitoring, threat triage, and investigation across your entire technology stack, ensuring threats are detected and validated any time, day or night.

Managed Detection & Response (MDR)

We go beyond simple alerting. Our MDR service combines proactive threat hunting with rapid, decisive action to contain and neutralize threats across your endpoints, cloud workloads, and network.

Managed Vulnerability Management

A proactive service to manage your attack surface. We continuously scan your environment, prioritize critical vulnerabilities based on risk, and work with your teams to manage the remediation lifecycle.

Incident Response Retainer

In the event of a major incident, you need expert guidance immediately. Our retainer service gives you priority access to our elite incident response team to help you navigate the crisis, from containment to recovery.

The Trescudo MSSP Advantage

We built the managed security service we always wanted: smarter, faster, and more integrated.

  • Platform-Powered Defence:

    Our SOC analysts are experts wielding the full power of our curated platform—from Orca's agentless cloud visibility to SentinelOne's autonomous response. This provides unmatched context and speed.

  • Deep Expertise on Demand:

    You gain immediate access to a team with over 20 years of experience and top-tier certifications, eliminating your hiring and retention challenges.

  • From Alert to Action, Fast:

    We don't just forward alerts. We investigate, validate, and respond. Our service is measured by outcomes—neutralized threats and reduced risk—not by the number of tickets closed.
