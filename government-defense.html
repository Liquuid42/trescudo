Cyber Resilience for EU Public Administration

A strategic framework and curated technology platform to help EU Government and Defence entities meet and exceed NIS2 compliance mandates.

The NIS2 Directive: A New Standard for Security

For the first time, the NIS2 Directive classifies public administration and defence organisations as "essential entities." This imposes legally-binding requirements for comprehensive cybersecurity risk management, supply chain security, and stringent incident reporting—all of which must be transposed into national law.

Management Accountability:

Leadership is now directly responsible for approving and overseeing cybersecurity risk-management measures.

Supply Chain Security:

You are required to address security-related risks in your own supply chains, including those of direct suppliers.

Strict Incident Reporting:

A multi-stage reporting process, including an initial notification within 24 hours of becoming aware of a significant incident.

A Technology Platform for NIS2 Compliance
