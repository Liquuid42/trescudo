Agentic AI Hyperautomation

The Agentic AI Core for Autonomous SecOps

Upgrade your Security Operations from reactive to autonomous. Introducing the hyperautomation platform that acts as the intelligent co-pilot for your security team.

Your Team is Overwhelmed. Your Tools Are Disconnected.

Security Operations (SecOps) teams face an impossible task: sifting through thousands of daily alerts from dozens of disconnected tools. This leads to severe alert fatigue, slow response times, and the constant risk of missing a critical threat.

11,000+ Daily Alerts:

The sheer volume of security alerts is unsustainable, leading to analyst burnout and missed incidents.

Slow Manual Triage:

Manual investigation is time-consuming and error-prone, dramatically increasing Mean Time to Respond (MTTR).

45% Effort on False Positives:

Nearly half of all investigation time is wasted on non-threats, distracting from genuine attacks.

Our Approach: From Automation to Autonomy with Agentic AI

Trescudo introduces **BrahmaFusion**, the Agentic AI and Hyperautomation platform from PerisAI. BrahmaFusion is not just another SOAR tool; it’s an intelligent co-pilot for your SOC. It uses a team of AI agents to autonomously triage and investigate alerts, correlate data across your entire security stack, and present human analysts with a complete incident summary and recommended actions.

This transforms your SecOps, reducing workload by over 35% and cutting MTTD from hours to minutes.

Key Features of BrahmaFusion

A vendor-agnostic platform designed to unify and automate your entire security ecosystem.

Autonomous Triage & Investigation

Let AI agents handle the heavy lifting. BrahmaFusion automatically ingests alerts, enriches them with context, and performs initial investigation steps, eliminating alert fatigue and focusing your team on what matters.

Hyperautomation Platform

Translate complex workflows into automated playbooks with an intelligent AI assistant. The no-code/low-code interface allows you to build, simulate, and deploy response actions in minutes.

Open Integration Hub

No vendor lock-in. BrahmaFusion connects to your existing security tools—SIEM, XDR, CSPM, and more—orchestrating actions across your entire ecosystem for a truly unified defence.

AI & Human Collaboration

BrahmaFusion is a co-pilot, not a replacement. AI agents provide insights and recommendations, but your human analysts always have the final say, combining machine speed with human intuition.

The Intelligent Core of the Trescudo Platform

BrahmaFusion acts as the central nervous system for the Trescudo portfolio, making every solution smarter and more effective.

  • Orchestrate Cloud Response:

    Automatically trigger actions based on alerts from Orca Security, such as isolating a compromised workload or revoking risky IAM permissions.

  • Automate Endpoint Containment:

    When SentinelOne detects a threat, BrahmaFusion can automatically disconnect the device from the network and query other tools for related activity.

  • Supercharge Managed Services:

    Our 24/7 SOC uses BrahmaFusion to deliver faster, more effective Managed Detection and Response (MDR), providing unparalleled value to our MSSP clients.
