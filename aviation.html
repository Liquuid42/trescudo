Ensuring Safety & Resilience from Ground to Cloud

Specialized Cybersecurity for the Modern Aviation Ecosystem—Airlines, Airports, and Supply Chain Partners.

Securing an Ecosystem Where There is No Margin for Error

The aviation industry is a high-stakes environment where digital and physical safety are intertwined. Protecting against threats requires deep expertise in both IT and Operational Technology (OT).

Protecting Flight Operations:

Securing flight-critical systems, Operational Technology (OT), and Air Traffic Control (ATC) communications.

Ensuring Regulatory Compliance:

Meeting stringent standards from authorities like EASA, ICAO, and national civil aviation bodies.

Securing the Supply Chain:

Managing risks from a complex network of maintenance providers, parts suppliers, and third-party contractors
